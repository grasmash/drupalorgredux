# Drupal Innovation Metrics — Verified Facts for Landing Page

Last updated: 2026-03-24

---

## Drupal CMS Release Timeline

| Milestone | Date |
|---|---|
| Starshot Initiative announced (DrupalCon Portland) | May 2024 |
| Drupal CMS 1.0 launched (Drupal's 24th birthday) | January 15, 2025 |
| Drupal CMS Desktop App announced (DrupalCon Atlanta) | April 2025 |
| Drupal Canvas 1.0 stable released | December 4, 2025 |
| Drupal CMS 2.0 launched (Drupal's 25th birthday) | January 28, 2026 |

**Key stat:** From Starshot announcement to CMS 1.0 = ~8 months. From CMS 1.0 to CMS 2.0 = ~12 months. Both major releases shipped on Drupal's birthday.

## Contribution & Community Growth

- **Organization credits tripled:** 54,089 (2019) to 151,475 (2024)
  - Source: Dries Buytaert, State of Drupal, DrupalCon Singapore, December 2024
  - https://dri.es/state-of-drupal-presentation-december-2024

- **2024 contribution growth (vs 2023):**
  - Organization credits for strategic initiatives grew **44%**
  - Individual contributions increased **37%**
  - Unique contributors rose **12.5%**
  - Participating organizations grew **11.3%**
  - Source: https://dri.es/drupal-cms-1-released

- **40% increase** in contributor activity since the Starshot initiative launched
- **2,000+ commits** from **300+ contributors** for Drupal CMS 1.0
- Drupal CMS 2.0 developed by **hundreds of contributors across dozens of organizations**

## Drupal AI Initiative

- **$1 million** fundraising goal reached in just **5 months** (June–October 2025)
  - Largest fundraising effort in Drupal's history
  - $200K in cash + $800K in committed FTE hours
  - **22 agency partners** backing the initiative
  - Source: https://www.drupal.org/about/starshot/initiatives/ai/blog/1-million-dollars-raised-to-accelerate-innovation-in-drupal-ai
- Drupal AI 1.2 stable released at DrupalCon Vienna (October 2025) — production-ready
- AI providers supported in CMS 2.0: amazee.ai Private AI, OpenAI, Anthropic

---

## Drupal Canvas (Visual Page Builder)

- Formerly "Experience Builder" — renamed to Drupal Canvas in 2025 (trademark issue)
- First shown as early prototype at DrupalCon Barcelona (September 2024)
- **15+ months** of development work
- Canvas 1.0 stable released **December 4, 2025**
- Default page builder in Drupal CMS 2.0 (January 2026)
- Sessions at DrupalCon Vienna were "standing room only"
- Source: https://www.drupal.org/blog/drupal-canvas-is-now-available-inside-drupals-new-visual-page-builder

---

## Drupal Recipes

- Recipes API added to Drupal core as experimental in **Drupal 10.3** (June 2024)
- Drupal CMS 1.0 shipped with **nearly 30 recipes** included
- Recipes are pre-configured packages of features (blogs, events, case studies, SEO, analytics, etc.)
- Source: https://www.drupal.org/docs/extending-drupal/drupal-recipes

---

## Performance (Drupal CMS 2.0 / Core 11.3)

- **26–33% more requests** with the same server setup
- Described as "the biggest performance improvement in a decade"
- Source: https://www.drupal.org/blog/drupal-cms-20-is-here-visual-building-ai-and-site-templates-transform-drupal

---

## Drupal CMS Desktop App (Launcher)

- Announced at DrupalCon Atlanta, **April 2025**
- Available for Mac (Windows pending app signing)
- No extra software required — built-in web server, SQLite database, and Drush
- Download: https://new.drupal.org/drupal-cms/launcher
- Source: https://www.drupal.org/about/starshot/blog/announcing-the-drupal-cms-desktop-application

---

## Site Templates

- "Byte" = first site template, included in Drupal CMS 2.0
  - Preconfigured SaaS marketing site (blog, newsletter, pricing, contact form)
  - **Installs in under 3 minutes**
  - Built with Canvas + Mercury component library
- Mercury component library includes cards, testimonials, heroes, menus, accordions
- Site Template Marketplace MVP proposed (mid-2025)

## Suggested Landing Page Copy (factual)

> **From zero to shipped in record time.** Drupal CMS 1.0 launched January 2025 — just 8 months after the Starshot initiative was announced. One year later, Drupal CMS 2.0 arrived with a visual page builder, AI tools, and site templates that install in under 3 minutes.

> **Community-powered momentum.** Organization contributions tripled from 54K to 151K credits between 2019 and 2024. Strategic initiative contributions grew 44% in a single year. The AI Initiative raised $1M in five months — the largest fundraising effort in Drupal's history.

> **Faster than ever.** Drupal CMS 2.0 on Core 11.3 delivers 26–33% more requests per server — the biggest performance gain in a decade.
