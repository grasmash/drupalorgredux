## Notes & Caveats

1. The **659,176 sites** figure only counts sites with the Update Status module enabled; actual Drupal usage is significantly higher.
2. The **1,000,000+ community members** figure comes from drupal.org's About page and likely refers to registered drupal.org accounts over the project's lifetime.
3. Contributor counts from Dries' analysis are from the 2020-2021 edition. More recent editions may exist but were not found at predictable URLs. The contribution *credits* data from the Dec 2024 State of Drupal keynote shows continued growth.
4. W3Techs data is as of March 2026.
5. Module/theme/distribution counts are live totals from drupal.org and include projects of all statuses (active, maintenance-only, etc.).
